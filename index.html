Interesting Mr. Robot Facts

  • It stars Rami Malek as Elliot Alderson, a cybersecurity engineer and hacker who suffers from social anxiety disorder and clinical depression.
  • Alderson is recruited by an insurrectionary anarchist known as “Mr. Robot”, played by Christian Slater, to join a group of hacktivists.
  • The group aims to cancel all debts by attacking the large corporation E Corp.
  • According to author Sam Esmail, he is fascinated by the hacker culture and wanted to make a film about it for around 15 years.
  • Another inspiration for Esmail, who is of Egyptian descent, was the Arab Spring, where young people who were angry at society used social media to bring about a change.
